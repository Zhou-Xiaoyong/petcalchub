About PetCalcHub

Free, science-backed pet calculators with AI-powered health insights.

Our Mission

PetCalcHub was built with a simple belief: every pet parent deserves access to reliable, science-backed tools to make informed decisions about their pet's health — without paywalls, signups, or data collection.

We combine veterinary-standard formulas from trusted sources — including AAHA, AAFCO, NRC, and Merck Veterinary Manual — with AI-powered insights that personalize each result for your specific pet. The result is a tool that doesn't just give you a number; it helps you understand what that number means and what to do next.

Why We're Different

🔬

Science First

Every formula is documented and sourced from peer-reviewed veterinary research. No guesswork, no "old wives' tales."

🤖

AI-Enhanced

Our AI engine generates personalized care tips based on your pet's specific results — breed, age, weight, and life stage.

🔒

Privacy First

All calculations happen in your browser. We never collect, store, or share your pet's information. No accounts, no tracking.

💰

Always Free

We believe essential pet health tools should be accessible to everyone. We're funded by advertising, not your data.

Our Sources

Our calculators draw from these authoritative veterinary sources:

  • AAHA (American Animal Hospital Association) — Canine and Feline Life Stage Guidelines
  • AAFCO (Association of American Feed Control Officials) — Nutritional guidelines
  • NRC (National Research Council) — Nutrient requirements for dogs and cats
  • Merck Veterinary Manual — Clinical reference
  • AAFP (American Association of Feline Practitioners) — Feline-specific guidelines
  • AVMA (American Veterinary Medical Association) — General veterinary standards

Medical Disclaimer

PetCalcHub provides educational estimates only. Our calculators are not a substitute for professional veterinary diagnosis, treatment, or advice. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical decisions about your pet's health. In case of emergency, contact your veterinarian or an animal poison control center immediately.
