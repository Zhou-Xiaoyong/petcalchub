Body Condition Score (BCS) Guide

The BCS is a 9-point scale used by veterinarians worldwide to assess pet body condition. It's more reliable than weight alone because it accounts for breed and frame size differences.

ScoreClassificationWhat You See/Feel% Over/Under Ideal
1-2UnderweightRibs, spine visible; no body fat20%+ under
3ThinRibs easily felt; waist obvious10-15% under
4-5IdealRibs felt with light pressure; visible waist0%
6-7OverweightRibs hard to feel; no visible waist10-20% over
8-9ObeseRibs not palpable; heavy fat deposits30%+ over

📏 How to Check BCS

  1. Rib check: Run hands along ribcage — should feel ribs with light pressure
  2. Waist check: Look from above — should see an hourglass waist behind ribs
  3. Abdominal tuck: Look from side — belly should tuck up behind ribcage
